# CarXplorer
A great app to check for the specs of your car
CarXplorer is your digital garage and ultimate car encyclopedia. Whether you’re a gearhead, a first-time buyer, or just curious about what’s under your hood, this app delivers detailed technical specifications for thousands of makes and models at your fingertips.From horsepower and torque to engine and prices, CarXplorer breaks down the data into easy-to-read charts.
